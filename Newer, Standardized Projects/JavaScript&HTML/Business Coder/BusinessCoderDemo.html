Column Responses
  • Group Address Details - Returns detailed address information, including Delivery Indicator and Plus4
  • Group Business Codes - Returns detailed business codes, including SIC Codes and NAICS Codes
  • Group Business Description - Returns detailed business descriptions, including SIC Descriptions and NAICS Descriptions
  • Group Geocode - Returns the geocoded latitude and longitude
  • Group Census - Returns detailed census information, including Census Block and Census Tract














Options
  • Centric Hint - Allows you to choose what the service will center the search around
  • Max Contacts - Changes the upper limit on the number of contacts returned
  • Return Dominant Business - Handles the returning of the dominant business name and demographics for a particular site when the company information is missing or does not match
  • SIC NAICS Confidence - If enabled, will only return SIC Codes that we are very confident are correct and return null otherwise
